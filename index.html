Sunchoke Superfoods: Grow, Eat, Heal

Your guide to Jerusalem Artichoke's prebiotic power, recipes, and sustainable growing.

The Sunchoke (or Jerusalem Artichoke) is a powerhouse of nutrition, rich in inulin, a prebiotic fiber essential for gut health. This tuber helps support a healthy microbiome and is a great source of natural energy.
